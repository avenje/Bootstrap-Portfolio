About Me

I am currently a Production Manager for a large printing company in Denver that has over 35 locations around the world.
I have been in the printing industry for almost 17 years and have decided to change gears and learn web development. I have solid experience working with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and Acrobat. Along with being the head Graphic Specialist at our location, I have great troubleshooting skills when it comes to repairing machines and solving problems.
I am currently enrolled in a 6-month program at the University of Denver for Web Development, and am hoping to find a job in the industry soon after receiving my certificate.
